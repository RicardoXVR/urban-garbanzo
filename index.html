"What is a 'Sleeper Agent'?"

A sleeper agent is a spy who is placed in a target country/ organization way before their mission begins. This allows them to blend in, passively collect intelligence and become potential assets once activated by the entity that deployed them.
They are essentially living and breathing trump cards, and having them in the field might be the difference between victory and defeat.

Do you know another species as omnipresent as birds? Didn't think so. And I wasn't kidding about the birds inside homes!

The bottom line is: Sleeper agents are always conveniently placed somewhere where it allows them to take action whenever necessary in a blink of an eye, and what is more convenient than being everywhere at the same time? Their network is insanely large!
